Carolyn (Carrie) Fahey is a PhD Candidate in the Department of Epidemiology at the University of California, Berkeley. Her research interests center on understanding and addressing social, economic, and environmental determinants of health.

Her current work involves designing, implementing, and evaluating scalable patient(client)-centered strategies to improve HIV and TB treatment adherence, including clinic-based financial incentives delivered using mobile health technology (mHealth) and targeted "cash plus" social support using predictive analytics.

She has experience managing data collection and analyzing data from randomized controlled trials, quasi-experimental studies, and longitudinal cohorts in the United States and sub-Saharan Africa.
